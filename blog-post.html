Why most Programmers work at Night

Published 3 Days ago5 min read18 comments

I am sure in saying that if you ask a random programmer when they do their best work and there’s a high chance they will admit to a lot of late nights. Some earlier, some later. A popular trend is to get up at 4 am and get some work done before the day’s craziness begins. Others like going to bed at 4 am.

Why then do we perform our most mentally complex work when the brain wants to sleep and we do simpler tasks when our brain is at its sharpest and brightest?

Because being tired makes us better coders!!!!

Being tired can make us focus better simply because when your brain is tired it has to focus! There isn’t enough left-over brainpower to afford losing concentration.

And when your brain isn’t working at full capacity it looks for something to do. Being tired makes you dumb enough that the task at hand is enough or else 90% of the time when we have energy we easily get lost in our crazy irrelavant thoughts

To conclude,Programmers work at night because it doesn’t impose a time limit on when you have to stop working, which gives you a more relaxed approach, your brain doesn’t keep looking for distractions and a bright screen keeps you awake.

Role of Sports in everyone's life!!

Sports should be considered as one of the important activities in our life. Normally education is important for a person to get in to a higher standard in the society. For that he or she has to study hard, similarly, sport is important to one’s life to keep their mind and body healthy.

It is a proven fact that people who are engaged in sport activities have more prevalence than the people who haven’t played or liked any sports. Besides playing any kind of sports is very useful for a person’s character as well. It leads you to gain courage, self-control and make your life more stress free.

Person who plays a sport will be knowing the lesson of the happiness of winning and also the pain of getting defeated, he or she will have the ability to accept the situation even when they lose or win equally, and move on with their life without giving up.

A sport can change a person’s life in to more meaningfulness and add some quality. When you become a sportsman you will be more responsible and also strong and fit.

Unless for the people who have decided to choose sports as their profession for others It is always hard to find time to engage even in small sports activities due to the busy life we spend trying to earn money. However, if you dedicate a small time of your life for sports then you will be able to live a different life consisting with more health and happiness.

Victory is in the quality of the competition, not just the final score....
